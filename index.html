    Slack Integration

    Connect CRIT 2 GO to your Slack workspace to send automated notifications at each workflow step.

    Create a webhook at api.slack.com → Your Apps → Incoming Webhooks
    #

    Setup Instructions

    1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and click Create New App → From Scratch
    2. Name your app "CRIT 2 GO" and choose your workspace
    3. Under Features → Incoming Webhooks, toggle it On
    4. Click Add New Webhook to Workspace and choose the #design-critique channel
    5. Copy the Webhook URL and paste it above
    6. CRIT 2 GO will now notify your team at every workflow step automatically
